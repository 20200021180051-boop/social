Who We Are

We are a group of students and researchers interested in understanding how social media is influencing Bhutanese culture, traditions, and lifestyle. Platforms like Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube are changing the way people communicate, dress, learn, and celebrate traditions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to raise awareness about both the positive and negative effects of social media in Bhutan. We believe technology can help preserve culture while also connecting Bhutanese youth to the modern world.

What We Focus On

  • Impact of social media on Bhutanese youth
  • Changes in traditional values and lifestyle
  • Promotion of Bhutanese culture online
  • Balancing modern technology with tradition
